Spicy Chicken Burgers With Mango Salsa

Description

Spicy Chicken Burgers with Mango Salsa is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines juicy, seasoned chicken
patties with a zesty mango salsa. The chicken patties are typically seasoned with a blend of spices, such as chili powder,
cumin, and paprika, giving them a spicy kick. These patties are then grilled or cooked until they're perfectly juicy and tender.

The star of this dish is the mango salsa, which adds a burst of freshness and sweetness. It's made by combining ripe mango chunks with
ingredients like red onion, cilantro, lime juice, and sometimes a touch of jalape√±o for a hint of heat. This salsa provides a vibrant contrast
to the spicy chicken, creating a harmonious balance of flavors.

When assembled in a bun, the spicy chicken patty is topped with a generous serving of mango salsa, creating a mouthwatering combination of spicy,
savory, and sweet tastes. It's a perfect choice for those who enjoy a bit of heat and a tropical twist in their burgers.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/3 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 1/3 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 lime, zested and juiced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • 5 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices gouda cheese
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • cup shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. Combine ground chicken, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, egg, lime zest, cilantro, garlic, and chipotle in a bowl. Shape mixture into four 1/4-pound patties.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Meanwhile, stir together chopped mangoes and the juice of the lime in a small bowl; set aside.
  4. Grill chicken burgers on the preheated grill until chicken is no longer pink inside, juices run clear, and burgers show browned grill marks, 5 to 8 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  5. About 2 minutes before burgers are done, place hamburger buns onto the grill to toast, about 1 minute per side. About 1 minute before the burgers are done, top each burger with a slice of Gouda cheese, and let melt.
  6. Spread each bun with 1 tablespoon mayonnaise and top with 1/4 cup shredded lettuce and grilled chicken burger. Spoon 1/4 of mango-lime mixture on each burger and top with the other half of the bun to serve.
